E-Cell Connect

E-Cell Connect is an initiative of nationwide connectivity. There are various engineering and management colleges which have independent Entrepreneurship Cells to support entrepreneurship. But there is a limit to the target audience they can reach because of the funds and manpower.

E-Cell Connect is a way to connect all these and help others in their events and other initiatives, to increase the cooperation and sharing of various resources.

Learning Program

To groom young enthusiasts, we provide learning sessions and discussion platforms. Peer to peer learning programs under the guidance of senior mentors, community meets to discuss various aspects of B2B and B2C companies along with solving of case studies in groups are scheduled on a weekly basis.
